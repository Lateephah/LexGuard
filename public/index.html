Disclaimer

LexGuard is a contract analysis tool designed to support users in identifying potentially important clauses within agreements.

This application does not provide legal advice and is not a substitute for a qualified legal professional.

The analysis is based on predefined rules and pattern matching, and may not detect all risks or nuances in a contract.

Users are strongly encouraged to consult a legal expert before making decisions.

LexGuard is intended to reduce cognitive load by guiding attention to important sections of a contract for further review.
